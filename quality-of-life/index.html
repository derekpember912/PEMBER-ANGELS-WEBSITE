Please complete this brief survey to evaluate your pet's current well-being. It should help you to determine whether now is the right time to consider next steps with compassion. After you complete this survey, if you determine that your pet is in distress (e.g., pain, cannot breathe), contact your local emergency hospital immediately. You can find a list of hospitals by clicking on the Hospitals tab at the top of the screen.
